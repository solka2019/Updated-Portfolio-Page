About Me

I am passionate about learning and teaching. I also enjoy traveling, walking my dog, and socializing with friends and family.

I've found it very rewarding to leverage my teaching skills to train others outside the classroom and in business oriented environments.

Learning new languages also fascinates me! It is a pleasure to serve as an interpreter especially in schools and medical environments. The favorite places that I've volunteered were Columbus and Seattle Literacy Council. After working with adults from Argentina and Mexico, I became interested in learning and later teaching Spanish to Americans and English to immigrants.

There is so much more we can learn from people when we are able to communicate in their native language!

When on vacation, I love going anywhere related to Disney! It is my desire to go to all of the Disney parks around the world.

In the meantime, my family and I have been hooked on Disney cruises! If not traveling, hiking with family and friends, and reading a good book are my next favorite hobbies.
